關於 ChiMeiRA

About ChiMeiRA

“Chimera” 源自於希臘神話，傳聞它上半身像獅子，中間像山羊，下半身是蛇；所以它也代表複合體的概念，後來醫學上也引用它來作為嵌合體。2025年『奇美月』活動結合了奇美醫院和奇美博物館，代表的是醫療和文化的嵌合。『雙管奇下』更是感染管制和品質管理的嵌合，因此研討會取名為 “ChiMeiRA”。，象徵醫療與文化的嵌合。本活動由品質管理部與感染管制中心共同發起，鏈結三院區與在地夥伴，打造高可靠、高韌性、以人為本的學習型系統。

“Chimera” originates from Greek mythology — a creature said to have the head of a lion, the body of a goat, and the tail of a serpent. The word thus came to symbolize a fusion or composite being, and it was later adopted in medicine to describe a chimeric cell or antibody. 2025 Chi Mei Month brings together Chi Mei Hospital and Chi Mei Museum, representing the fusion of medicine and culture. The theme “雙管奇下” further reflects the integration of Infection Control and Quality Management, which is why the symposium is named “ChiMeiRA.” Co‑hosted by the Departments of Quality Management and Infection Control Center, it connects three campuses and local partners to build a high‑reliability, resilient, people‑centred learning system.
